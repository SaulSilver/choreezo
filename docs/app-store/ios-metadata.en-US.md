## Description

Choreezo makes chore planning simple for shared homes.

Create or join an apartment, view the weekly schedule, and claim chores in seconds. Everyone sees what is assigned, what is still open, and what is due today.

With daily reminders and weekly summaries, your group can stay consistent without constant chat messages or manual spreadsheets.

What you can do in Choreezo:
- Create or join an apartment with an invite code.
- Browse chores by day in a weekly schedule.
- Claim unassigned chores or reassign tasks.
- Track your own tasks in My Chores.
- Manage reminders from Settings.

Choreezo is built for roommates who want a fair and lightweight way to share household responsibility.
